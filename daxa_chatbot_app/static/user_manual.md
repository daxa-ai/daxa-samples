# Personal Financial Analysis App

## User Manual & Quick Start Guide

---

# Page 1

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to the Personal Financial Analysis App. This application helps users track income, monitor expenses, analyze spending patterns, manage budgets, and improve overall financial health. The platform is designed for individuals and families who want better visibility into their finances.

### Core Features

* Income and expense tracking
* Monthly and yearly financial reports
* Category-wise spending analysis
* Budget planning and alerts
* Savings and investment tracking
* Secure cloud-based data storage

---

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Account Registration

1. Open the application.
2. Click on **Sign Up**.
3. Enter your email address and password.
4. Verify your email using the OTP or verification link.
5. Complete your profile setup.

### 2.2 Login Process

Existing users can log in using their registered email and password. Multi-factor authentication may be enabled for additional security.

> **Tip:** Use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication for enhanced account protection.

## 4. Managing Financial Data

### 4.1 Adding Income

1. Navigate to the **Income** section.
2. Click **Add Income**.
3. Enter source, amount, date, and notes.
4. Save the transaction.

### 4.2 Recording Expenses

1. Go to the **Expenses** tab.
2. Select expense category.
3. Enter amount and transaction details.
4. Attach receipt if needed.
5. Click **Save**.

### Popular Expense Categories

* Food & Dining
* Transportation
* Utilities
* Healthcare
* Entertainment
* Education

---

## 5. Budget Planning

Users can create monthly budgets to control spending and improve savings.

### How to Create a Budget

1. Open the **Budget Planner**.
2. Select a month and year.
3. Assign spending limits to categories.
4. Save the budget.

The application will notify users when spending exceeds predefined limits.

> **Tip:** Start with realistic budget goals and review them monthly for better financial discipline.
